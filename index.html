Ride with confidence.
Learn with kindness.

Lessons for absolute beginners through to advanced riders, with quiet lanes, woodland tracks, and a friendly arena at our ranch‑style yard.

Beginner‑friendly Group & Private Children & Adults

Lessons & Programmes

From first trot to canter, our coaches tailor each session to your goals, confidence and style.

Discovery Ride

Perfect for first‑timers. Balance, posture and basic aids in a calm setting.

£35 / 30 mins

Arena Foundations

Steering, transitions & control. Great for riders moving towards canter.

£55 / 60 mins

Trail Confidence

Guided hacks on lanes and tracks. Learn steady pace & road sense.

£60 / 75 mins

Why Deanswood

  • Kind, confidence‑building coaching
  • Horses matched carefully to riders
  • Hats & body protectors available
  • After‑school clubs & camps
  • Gift vouchers

About Deanswood

We’re a friendly, family‑run yard with a ranch feel — open spaces, calm horses and a kettle that’s always on.

We believe good riding is kind riding: patient tuition, steady progress and celebrating every little win.

Meet the herd

Star, Bramble, Juno & Ash — our gentle teachers with big hearts and bigger hay nets.
