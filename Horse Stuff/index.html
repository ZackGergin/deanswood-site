About Willow Ridge

Born from a love of horses and teaching, Willow Ridge is a small, family‑run school on the edge of town. Our yard is quiet, our fields are wide, and our kettle is always on.

We believe good riding is kind riding — for horses and humans alike. That means patient tuition, gradual goals, and celebrating every little win (first rising trot, anyone?).
